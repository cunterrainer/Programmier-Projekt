The storm raged outside, relentless and fierce. Thunderclaps echoed through the night, shaking the very foundations of the old house. Rain lashed against the windows, creating a constant drumming sound that filled the air. Lightning lit up the sky in sporadic bursts, casting eerie shadows that danced across the walls. Inside, the family huddled together by the fireplace, seeking warmth and comfort amidst the chaos outside. The flickering flames cast a warm, golden glow, a stark contrast to the cold, unforgiving storm. They spoke in hushed tones, their voices barely audible over the storm's roar, as they shared stories to pass the time and distract themselves from the tempest's fury
